Enhanced Picture-in-Picture Experience

Watch videos with a floating window that stays on top of other applications, complete with playback controls and seamlessly integrated subtitles for a superior viewing experience.

Features

Playback controls will be displayed so you can:

  • Play/Pause the video
  • Adjust/Mute the volume
  • Display subtitles (currently works on Youtube, Netflix, Disney+ and PrimeVideo)

Frequently Asked Questions

The Subtitle-PiP Extension is a browser extension that enables subtitles in Picture-in-Picture (PiP) mode for popular streaming platforms like Netflix and YouTube. Enjoy uninterrupted multitasking while staying engaged with on-screen dialogue.

If you often multitask while watching videos in Picture-in-Picture mode, this extension is perfect for you! It ensures you can follow along with subtitles even when the video is minimized, enhancing accessibility and convenience.

Currently, the Subtitle-PiP Extension supports Netflix and YouTube. We are actively working to expand compatibility to other streaming platforms in the future.
